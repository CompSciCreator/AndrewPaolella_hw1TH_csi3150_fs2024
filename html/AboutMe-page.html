My Favorite Book:

12 Rules for Life

Purchase: Buy on Amazon

I love this book because it provides great advice about life, structured in a way that encourages personal responsibility. Offering rules organised around the idea of fighting the chaos that constantly threatens to engulf our lives, we must find ways to live with it and create order. Jordan uses personal anecdotes, intellectual history, physchology and religious imagery. Each rule provides insights that provoke deep thought about how we approach everyday life challenges.
